## Welcome to Applied AI for Everybody

This interactive textbook is designed to help non-STEM students understand, test, and effectively use AI tools in their creative processes. Whether you're a writer, musician, artist, or any other kind of creator, this guide will help you navigate the world of AI assistants.

## What You'll Learn

	<Card title="AI Tool Testing" icon="magnifier">
		Learn why and how to test AI tools for your specific needs.
	</Card>
